About

Our Global CO2 Analysis Project unveils the story of carbon dioxide emissions across the globe. Utilizing the power of Tableau, we bring CO2 data to life with interactive visualizations, empowering you to explore trends by country, sector, and time. These insights, presented through clear visuals and commentary, paint a comprehensive picture of our climate landscape. This project is a gateway to understanding CO2's impact, sparking informed conversations and paving the way for a data-driven future.

Together, let's turn this knowledge into action for a healthier planet.
